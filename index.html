About Me

Hey there! I’m Scott from Louisiana. Once upon a time, I was a certified piece of shit—yes, you read that right. But everyone loves a good redemption arc, right?


Now, I’ve swapped my old ways for dumbbells, tech books, and the endless awkwardness that is my charm. I’ve become a fitness enthusiast, social butterfly (with a twist of social awkwardness), and the guy who will always have a random fact or two about coding to share.


Speaking of which, I’m currently diving deep into the world of coding and building websites. It’s a wild ride, but hey, it beats being my old self. Stick around to see what I come up with next. Cheers!


Feel free to join me on my cosmic journey as we explore the vast unknown together!
